ARK: Survival Evolved is one of those games that can be both amazing and frustrating simultaneously. HALO: THE MASTER CHIEF COLLECTION

Recommendation:
★★★★⯨

Halo: The Master Chief Collection is an outstanding collection that brings together some of the most iconic FPS campaigns ever made into one polished package. The storytelling—especially in Halo 2 and Halo 3—still feels powerful and cinematic, and the remastered visuals add a fresh layer to the classic experience. Multiplayer delivers a massive variety of beloved maps and modes, offering endless replay value for both longtime fans and newcomers. Although it had a rough launch years ago and the interface can feel a bit cluttered at times, the collection has improved tremendously. Overall, it’s an essential experience for anyone who appreciates classic, genre-defining shooters.
